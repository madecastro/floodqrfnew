Good documentation makes claims smoother.

## Most helpful items
- Photos before, during, and after mitigation
- Moisture readings and daily logs
- Notes on affected materials and scope
- Clear work descriptions aligned to restoration standards

We can provide the documentation you need to reduce back-and-forth.
